Siavash

One day, two Iranian heroes, Guiv and Toos went hunting close to the country of Touran. They were in the meadow. Suddenly a beautiful girl appeared where Guiv and Toos were trying to hunt. They asked her, why she was there and she said that her father mistreated her and she ran away to the meadow. Both heroes fell in love with the beautiful girl and started fighting against one another. Finally, they decided to go to King kaykavoos and ask him to decided which one of them would be the beautiful girl’s husband. When King Kaykavoos saw the girl, he also fell in love with her. King Kaykavoos told Guiv and Toos that the beautiful girl should marry the king. 9 months after their wedding,, the beautiful queen gave birth to a boy named Siavash. Unfortunately, the queen passed away right after she gave birth to Siavash. Traditionally in every kingdom, the astrologist or fortune tellers would tell the king how the new born would turn out to be. Unfortunately the fortune tellers didn’t see Siavash’s future bright. Rostam told King Kaykavoos that he would raise Siavash to be a hero. Rostam taught Siavash to be a great warrior up to the age of seventeen (17). At the age of 17th, Siavash told Rostam that he wanted to go back to his father, Kaykavoos. When king Kaykavoos saw Suavash, he couldn’t believe what a great warrior he had turned out to be. Siavash was so handsome that even his step mother, Sudabeh fell in love with him.
